Experimental register

What this page records—and what comes next.

Implemented

Single- and multi-layer schedules, complete-candidate scoring, free generation, per-layer propagation capture, and matched-norm random controls.

Required before claims

A distribution of random controls, reverse Laurel-target schedules, frequency-filtered source variants, and strength/window scans fixed before evaluation.

Interpretation boundary: the encoder has no vocabulary distribution. These edits are residual-space interventions proposed by the J-lens and evaluated by actual downstream computation. They do not show that Whisper—or people—represent phonemes as “L” versus “Y.”
